Veg Fried Rice

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20-25 minutes
  • Serves: 2
  • Ingredients
    • For Cooking Rice
      • 2 cups Sella basmati rice, soaked for 20 minutes
      • Water as required
      • Salt to taste
      • ½ tbsp Oil
    • For Fried Rice
      • 1 tbsp Oil,
      • 2-4 Garlic cloves, chopped
      • ½ inch Ginger, chopped
      • 2 Green chilli, chopped
      • 2 large Carrot, peeled, chopped
      • 1/4 Cabbage chopped fine (optional )
      • Salt to taste
      • ½ tsp Sugar
      • 1 tbsp Dark soy sauce
      • Cooked Rice
      • ¼ inch Ginger, chopped
      • 1 Green chilli, chopped
      • 8-10 French beans, chopped
      • 2 medium size Capsicum, chopped
      • 1 ½ tsp Vinegar
      • 2 tsp Green chilli sauce
      • ½ tsp White pepper powder
      • ¼ cup Spring onion, chopped
      • 1 tbsp Coriander leaves, chopped
  • Process
    • For Cooking Rice
      • In a pot, add water for boiling, salt to taste, oil and let it boil.
      • Once the water starts boiling. Add soaked sella basmati rice and let it cook on medium heat until 70% cooked.
      • Once done, strain the rice and keep aside for further use.
    • For Fried Rice
      • Heat oil in a wok, once it's hot, add garlic, ginger, green chillies and saute it well.
      • Add carrot, salt to taste, sugar, dark soy sauce and cook for 2-4 minutes.
      • Stir fry on high flame without overcooking vegetables.
      • Add cooked rice and saute it well. add ginger, green chilli and toss it well. (Add canbage at this stage if using )
      • Stir-fry until the sauce is combined well.
      • Add french beans, capsicum, vinegar, green chilli sauce, white pepper powder and mix it well.
      • Now, add spring onion, coriander leaves and toss everything well.
      • Garnish with spring onion and serve hot.
